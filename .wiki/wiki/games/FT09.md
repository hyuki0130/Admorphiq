## Observations

- Grid of clickable cells; clicking toggles the clicked cell and neighbors
- Constraints (color targets) printed on designated cells
- Classic lights-out variant over GF(p) for some p

## Mechanics Hypothesis

A lights-out / XOR-toggle puzzle where each click modifies the clicked cell plus a neighborhood; the goal configuration is encoded by constraint sprites. Solvable analytically via linear algebra over the small finite field.
